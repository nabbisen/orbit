scanner, extraction, chunking, keyword search. All implemented.

RFC-008 (embeddings), RFC-009 (hybrid RRF). Both implemented.

## Part 3 — Product UX and Operations (v0.4–v0.5)
RFC-010 (reranking), RFC-011 (storage dashboard), RFC-012 (model registry),
RFC-013 (search UX), RFC-014 (Japanese search). All implemented.

## Part 4 — Hardening (v0.5)
RFC-015 (security), RFC-016 (benchmarks), RFC-017 (packaging),
RFC-018 (crash recovery). All implemented.

## Part 4 — Release Readiness (v0.6)
RFC-019 (test matrix), RFC-020 (documentation). Target v0.6.

## Part 5 — Deferred Future Work (v0.7+)

archives, XLSX/PPTX, plugins, model trust, portable mode.
